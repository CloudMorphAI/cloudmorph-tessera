# The Team

## Sainath — Founder

Sainath is the sole founder of CloudMorph AI and the primary builder of Tessera. He works
full-time at Telus in cloud and infrastructure, and builds Tessera in the evenings and on
weekends.

The infrastructure background is not incidental. Tessera was built because Sainath ran into
the problem it solves — MCP tool calls operating outside any policy boundary — while working
on cloud infrastructure automation. The tool exists because the author needed it.

Building evenings and weekends while employed full-time means the project moves slower than
a funded team, but also means it is not dependent on investor timelines or runway pressure.
The decisions are made by someone with direct exposure to the problem, not by a team
optimizing for growth metrics.

## Sneha — Advisor

Sneha is Sainath's sister and an advisor and supporter to the project. She is not a
technical co-founder and is not involved in day-to-day development. Her involvement is in
the early-stage clarity that any founder needs: pressure-testing ideas, identifying what the
project is actually about, and providing honest feedback when the direction drifts.

## CloudMorph AI

CloudMorph AI is a small, bootstrapped team with a focus on cloud infrastructure tooling.
Tessera is the company's first open-source project. CloudMorph also builds a cloud
infrastructure management console as its primary commercial product — Tessera grew out of
work on that product and is being developed in parallel.

## What this team is not

At launch, this is a solo project. There is no team of ten engineers, no design team, no
dedicated DevRel. The handbook, the documentation, and the code are all built by one person
with AI assistance.

We think that is worth saying plainly. It affects what we can commit to, how fast we can
respond to issues, and what our support looks like in the early months. We would rather be
honest about that now than create expectations we cannot meet.
